    How To Play

    ×
    • Put your guess for the capital city into the What Do You Think box.
    • Your guess can be typed, or selected from the dropdown list.
    • Either way, your guess much match one of the options from the dropdown list.
    • If you type the first letter of your guess, the dropdown list automatically will show you all the options that start with that letter.
    • Alternatively, if you want to see all options (every capital city in the region of the quiz), click the dropdown arrow to the right of the What Do You Think box.
    • When you have your guess, click the Check Answer button to get feedback.
    • When you're ready, click the Next button to bring up the next country.
    • Enjoy :)
